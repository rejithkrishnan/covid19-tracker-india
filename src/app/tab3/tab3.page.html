FAQ FAQ What is this app? This is a mobile app for tracking COVID19 status in India. All data is sourced from APIs provided by covid19india.org. Please visit thier visit at covid19india.org for more detailed statistics Are you official? No What are your data sources? Data is updated as and when covid19india.org database is updated. covid19india.org are using state bulletins and official handles to update our numbers. The data is validated by a group of volunteers and published into a Google sheet and an API Why does covid19india.org have more positive count than MoH? MoHFW updates the data at a scheduled time. However, we update them based on state press bulletins, official handles and reliable news channels which could be more recent. Some useful links MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE, GOV.
